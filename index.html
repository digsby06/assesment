About B8ta

While b8ta keeps the advertising offline, they continue to track everything online. Products get real life versions of online ads—

instead of clicks to websites, partners get physical encounters with customers for a measured period of time.

They put products on display out of the box, that allow partners to digital message, and then measure the in-person interactions visitors have with it.

Every product’s digital display offers all the information customers would typically use their smartphone to research.

They encourage in-person discovery and hands-on experience. At b8ta, their primary aim is to increase awareness of brands and the recognition of products.
